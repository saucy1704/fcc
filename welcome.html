G-Wagon Nation

The G-Wagon (from Geländewagen, meaning cross-country vehicle) has retained its instantly recognizable, military-inspired silhouette for over four decades. Handcrafted in Graz, Austria, each vehicle maintains a tough ladder-frame chassis that allows it to conquer extreme terrain while offering a lavish, highly customizable interior.
